Transforming Transfer Enrollment and Budget Processes at Rutgers University-Newark

The Problem

Rutgers University-Newark (RU-N) is one of the regional campuses of Rutgers University, a distinguished public land-grant research institution with three campuses in New Jersey. Located in the heart of Newark, RU-N offers a diverse and vibrant academic environment.

The Action

To address declining transfer enrollment and other enrollment and budget-related issues, RU-N embarked on a transformative journey with the help of the NACUBO grant-funded project. RU-N utilized the Budget Process Assessment to gain insights into stakeholder knowledge gaps regarding the budget process. This collaboration disrupted silos and fostered open, honest conversations about cultural changes needed to connect student outcomes to budget decisions.

The Results

With the implementation of a new transfer evaluation team and center, RU-N decreased the transfer credit evaluation time from several weeks to 1 to 2 days.
The development of an enrollment dashboard provided diverse stakeholders with centralized access to enrollment and financial data.
RU-N launched a SharePoint site as a centralized communication hub, offering comprehensive information on budget processes and guidelines.
