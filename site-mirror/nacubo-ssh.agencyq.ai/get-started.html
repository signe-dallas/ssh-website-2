New Here?

The NACUBO Student Success Hub is a free online resource center that provides higher education institutions with tools, frameworks, and best practices to improve student success through strategic finance, budgeting, and data-informed decision-making.

How the Hub Works

The hub brings together practical resources, case studies, toolkits, and guidance to help colleges and universities improve student outcomes.

It is free to use, and NACUBO membership is not required.

Create an Account

  • Save and quickly access the tools you need.
  • Bookmark toolkits, find assessments, action plans, and metrics dashboards.
  • Connect with NACUBO subject matter experts.

Get Guidance

  • Connect with higher education professionals facing similar challenges.
  • Explore real institutional examples and practical implementation strategies.
  • Get support, guidance, and ideas you can apply to your campus goals.

Use Public Resources

  • Browse available toolkits without creating an account.
  • Read case studies, reports and blogs.
  • Listen to webinars and podcasts.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Student Success Hub provides practical, research-backed toolkits designed to help institutions align their financial and operational decisions with student success outcomes. It offers frameworks, templates, and guidance to support your institution’s implementation efforts.
Login ensures that toolkit resources are accessed by authorized institutional representatives, helping NACUBO maintain the quality and integrity of member-exclusive content and track engagement with the platform.
After logging in, we recommend starting with the Toolkit Finder assessment to identify which toolkits best match your institution’s needs and priorities. You can then explore recommended toolkits and begin implementing their resources.
