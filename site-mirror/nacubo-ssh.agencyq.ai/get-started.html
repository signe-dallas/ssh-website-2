How the Hub Works

The NACUBO Student Success Hub is a free online resource center that helps higher education institutions improve student success through practical tools, frameworks, case studies, and data-informed strategies.

Student Success Focus Areas

Data and Decision-Making

Access resources that support data visibility, reporting, accountability, and informed decision-making.

Financial Resource Optimization

Explore strategies for evaluating resource allocation and aligning investments with student success goals.

Student-Centered Planning and Budgeting

Find guidance for planning, budgeting, and operational strategies that improve student outcomes.

What You'll Find in the Hub

The Hub brings together practical resources, implementation guidance, and institutional examples that institutions can use individually or as part of broader student success efforts.

They are free to use, and NACUBO membership is not required.

  2. Explore relevant resources and guidance

Ways to Use the Hub

Find and use free resources, save helpful tools, and connect with guidance designed to support your institution's student success goals.

Save and Organize

Create a free account to save resources, bookmark helpful content, and quickly return to the tools your team needs.

  • Save and quickly access resources.
  • Bookmark toolkits, find assessments, action plans, and metrics dashboards.
  • Organize resources for future use.

Get Guidance

Connect with peers, explore real examples, and find support you can apply to your goals.

  • Connect with higher education professionals facing similar challenges.
  • Explore real institutional examples and practical implementation strategies.
  • Get support, guidance, and ideas you can apply to your campus goals.

Use Public Resources

Use free resources available to all visitors, no account required.

  • Search available toolkits.
  • Read case studies, reports and blogs.
  • Listen to webinars and podcasts.
