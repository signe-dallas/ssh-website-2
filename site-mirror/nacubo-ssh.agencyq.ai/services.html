How Our Services Work

Institutions participate at the phase that aligns with their needs, using a flexible, research-based framework that adapts to their goals and capacity.

Explore

Assess current state and identify opportunities for improvement.

Explore

Stakeholder interviews, environmental scan, data review, benchmarking analysis, and opportunity mapping.

Ideate

Generate and evaluate potential approaches and solutions.

Ideate

Design thinking workshops, brainstorming sessions, best practice research, solution prioritization, and feasibility assessment.

Plan and Build

Design actionable plans with clear ownership and timelines.

Plan and Build

Project planning, resource allocation, timeline development, toolkit customization, and stakeholder alignment.

Test and Learn

Pilot approaches with rapid feedback loops for refinement.

Test and Learn

Pilot design, success metrics, feedback collection, iterative refinement, and lessons-learned documentation.

Implement

Launch coordinated actions and monitor early progress.

Implement

Change management, training delivery, communication plans, progress monitoring, and barrier resolution.

Integrate

Embed practices into ongoing operations and decision cycles.

Integrate

Sustainability planning, policy integration, ongoing measurement, knowledge transfer, and continuous improvement.
