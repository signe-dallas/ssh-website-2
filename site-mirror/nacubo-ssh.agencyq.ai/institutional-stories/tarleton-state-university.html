Applying Data Science to Business Decisions at Tarleton State University

The Problem

Tarleton State University, located in Stephenville, Texas, is a vibrant institution known for its commitment to academic excellence. Founded in 1899, Tarleton is a member of the Texas A&M University system and has other campus locations in Fort Worth, Waco, and Bryan. With a current enrollment of nearly 17,000 students.

The Action

In 2021-2022, Tarleton State University initiated a strategic effort to improve its benchmarking capabilities by establishing an IPEDS Data Evaluation Task Force. The task force utilized a combination of national data and a longitudinal study to create a comprehensive and data-informed benchmarking framework. Tarleton customized the IPEDS Peer Benchmarking Dashboards tool to focus solely on the institution and its selected peer and aspirant groups.

The Results

Through collaboration with NACUBO, Tarleton adopted and customized the IPEDS Peer Benchmarking Dashboards, expanding its Power BI capabilities to include tailored visualizations focused on key variables related to student success.
The adoption of the customized Power BI dashboards has facilitated enhanced data literacy, supported data storytelling, and promoted data-informed decision-making across the university.
