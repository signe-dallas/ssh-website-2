IPEDS Peer Benchmarking Dashboards

The IPEDS Peer Benchmarking solution empowers Chief Business Officers (CBOs) and other institutional leaders to identify key data trends, understand the potential institutional impacts of such trends, and translate data into meaningful action. Leveraging pre-populated Power BI dashboards, this comprehensive tool provides a strong data foundation for leaders, regardless of institutional level, sector, or background. The dashboards help stakeholders better understand their own data and assist in developing a roadmap for contextualized data-informed decision-making.

Launch Toolkit

Value Proposition

Assists institutions in visualizing their institutional and peer data across key higher education metrics.

Aids institutions in understanding inherent data patterns.

Prompts facilitation of organizational learning and improvement based on data findings.

Implementation Options

Choose the level of adoption that fits your institution's capacity and goals. Every toolkit offers flexible implementation paths from quick wins to comprehensive integration.

Low Lift— Quick Start

Review the pre-populated Power BI dashboards to access fundamental data trends without extensive customization efforts.

Mid Lift— Deeper Integration

Engage in strategic conversations to interpret institutional and peer data patterns, facilitating informed decision-making.

High Lift— Full Implementation

Customize the IPEDS Peer Benchmarking dashboards to tailor data analysis to specific institutional needs and priorities.
